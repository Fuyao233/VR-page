VR Writing



Abstract

The project aims to simulate a classroom in examination with Unity 3D and to answer the paper with motion capture gloves. To simulate more realistically, we add more details such as teachers and other students based on the demo scene. Simultaneously, we set a pen as an interactive object and build connection with the motion capture gloves and virtual reality glasses. In the end, we can write on the paper.

Design Ideas

  • Immersion

    In order to reproduce the pressure that students may meet in exam, we set two proctor teachers as Chinese tradition. The first proctor teacher sits in front of the classroom serving as the chief proctor. And the vice proctor walks around, inspecting the classroom. Additionally, we place a clock on the wall, which shows the time and plays the countdown audio when “test time” is fifteen minutes left. Finally, we set a background music containing sounds of turning paper and writing, and then built an immersive scenery of real exam.

    fig. Some details to enhance atmosphere

  • Interaction

    We improve the interaction mainly from three aspects. Firstly, we found that it may be difficult for player to hold the pen, so we adjust the pen a little bit bigger. Now player can hold the object conveniently. Secondly, we found that since the size of pen had changed, the accuracy of writing reduced, as resolution, we adjust the ray casts by pen, and make the check point just in the top of the pen. Lastly, we find the vestige of handwriting in the board sometimes on and off, so we make an insertion between the check position on the board, and ensure player can writing fluently.

  • Future work

    Yet we haven't assigned the animation of turning paper on NPC in the scene, but it could improve the immersion experience indeed. What's more, the color of the pen are set as black, and can only be changed in the inspector windows, we plan to set few button on board or pen, then could change the color freely. And the eraser’s effect of cleaning writing is still the same as the pen, which could be improved by setting the checking point to checking surface.

  • Device Adjustment

    Device list: VIVE PRO EYE headset & Noitom 2.0. The experience of device adjustment we conclude is that, you have to distinguish between left and right tracker, otherwise you will receive a wrong track. From another point, it’s highly recommended that you use the motion capture glove when its power is sufficient, for gaining a better tracking result.
